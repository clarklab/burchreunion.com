Welcome

The 2023 Burch Family Reunion will take place July 14-16 at the Innsbrook Village Country Club & Resort in Ruidoso, New Mexico! We'd love for you to join us, and hope this page has all the information needed to make your trip an easy one.

Schedule

The schedule is coming soon! At Innsbrook, we have the main clubhouse (for meals, getting together, playing 42), along with a fishing pond, golf course, tennis and basketball courts, and a heated pool. There are trails right across the street.

If you'd like to help with the schedule or suggest activities, send us an email.

Meals

Meal planning is underway! Right now, we're planning to have a catered Friday Dinner and a catered Saturday Dinner in the main Innsbrook Clubhouse (we have this building all day July 14-16).

If you'd like to help with meals or suggest lunch or breakfast plans, send us an email.

Lodging

This year, we're staying at Innsbrook Village Country Club & Resort on July 14-16. You can book directly with Innsbrook on their website or by calling 575-258-3589.

When you book, please send us an email or message so we can keep an accurate headcount (for meals, activities, etc).

Most of their units are multi-bedroom and can easily sleep 4-10 people, so you might want to pair up with other family to get the best deal.

Innsbook offers fishing from their pond, free golf, tennis and basketball courts, a heated pool, and more. Check their website for more information.

Currently, they have plenty of room to hold our entire group, but it'd be best to book ASAP to make sure we all secure space.

For RV and Camping, try Recreation Village, about 5 minutes up the road.

For other options, try Village Lodge or Comfort Inn Midtown. There are plenty of other hotels in the Ruidoso area. All group events will be hosted at Innsbook, but you are welcome to stay anywhere you like.

If you have any questions or need help booking, you can send us an email.
